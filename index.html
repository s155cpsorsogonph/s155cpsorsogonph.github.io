Welcome to S155CP Sorsogon Chapter!

Who We Are


S155CP (Sniper 155 Club of the Philippines) is a vibrant motorcycle club dedicated to Yamaha Sniper 155 enthusiasts. We’re not just riders; we’re a family that thrives on the thrill of the ride and the connections we build along the way. Our club embodies the values of camaraderie, unity, trust, and respect, creating a welcoming environment for all members. Together, we celebrate our shared passion for riding, supporting one another both on and off the road. Whether you’re a seasoned rider or just starting out, S155CP is your home for friendship, adventure, and unforgettable experiences. Join us as we explore the open road and forge lasting bonds within our community!

We as riders

WE RIDE AS ONE

S155CP Bicol Sector brings together Yamaha Sniper 155 riders from Albay, Sorsogon, Camarines Norte, Camarines Sur and Masbate. We celebrate the thrill of motorcycling while fostering camaraderie and adventure among our members. Together, we embrace the spirit of the ride and the beauty of Bicolandia!
